PROFILE IMAGE SETUP
===================

Please save your professional profile image as:
  src/assets/profile.jpg

The image you provided should be saved with this exact filename.

Image Requirements:
- Filename: profile.jpg
- Location: src/assets/
- Format: JPG/JPEG
- Recommended size: 800x800px or higher (square ratio works best)
- The component will automatically crop it to circular shape

Once you save the image, the About section will display your professional photo with:
- Circular frame with gradient border
- Animated rotating glow effect
- Hover animations
- "Deep Tech Entrepreneur" badge at the bottom
- Professional shadow and lighting effects

The design is fully responsive and will look great on all devices!
